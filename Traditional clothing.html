Basotho traditional attire

Seshoeshoe:Seshoeshoe, also known as Shweshwe, is a distinctive fabric traditionally worn by Basotho women. This vibrant, colorful fabric is typically made of cotton print and symbolizes unity and pride among Basotho women. Seshoeshoe dresses are often worn during important cultural ceremonies and celebrations
